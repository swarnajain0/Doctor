About Dr. Harshit Dave

Full Endoscopic & Complex Spine Specialist
MBBS, MS(Ortho, Mumbai), DNB, FISS, FMISS, FESS

Dr. Harshit Dave is one of India’s most trusted and top-rated spine surgeon, known not only for his surgical expertise but also for his deep compassion and patient-first philosophy. With a strong belief in evidence-based, minimally invasive care, Dr. Dave has helped thousands of patients regain mobility and live pain-free lives.

Early Life and Inspiration

Born and raised in Madhya Pradesh, Dr. Dave showed a remarkable interest in medical science from a young age. His journey into orthopaedics and spine surgery was driven by a deep desire to heal and improve the quality of life for others. That same passion continues to guide his work today.

Professional Journey

Dr. Dave's career has been marked by academic excellence and rigorous training. He is board-certified and has completed advanced fellowships that have equipped him with the expertise to manage a wide range of spinal conditions, from common issues like disc herniation and sciatica to complex deformities and spine trauma.

He is especially skilled in endoscopic and minimally invasive spine procedures, which allow for faster recovery, minimal postoperative pain, and lower complication rates. These advanced techniques have revolutionized the way spine care is delivered and Dr. Dave is at the forefront of this transformation in India.

Patient-Centered Philosophy

Dr. Dave firmly believes that most spine problems do not require surgery. His approach emphasizes conservative, non-operative treatments wherever possible. When surgery is necessary, it is performed with the utmost precision, using the least invasive methods available.

Patients consistently commend him for his empathetic communication, personalized care, and transparency. Whether it’s taking extra time to explain a diagnosis or walking a patient through each step of treatment, Dr. Dave ensures that patients feel informed, reassured, and cared for.

Education & Specialized Training

  • MBBS – Government Medical College & SSG Hospital, Vadodara
  • MS in Orthopaedics – Grant Medical College & JJ Hospital, Mumbai
  • DNB Orthopaedics – National Board of Examinations
  • Fellowship in Spine Surgery – The Spine Foundation, Mumbai
    (Training at Lilavati Hospital, Breach Candy Hospital, Sir HN Reliance Foundation Hospital)
  • Advanced Endoscopic Spine Surgery Training – Asian Spine Hospital, Hyderabad

In addition to his academic credentials, Dr. Dave has worked as a spine specialist in Ahmedabad, gaining extensive hands-on experience in the latest advancements in spinal care.
